## Overview

Bridge loans allow homeowners to access equity from their current home before selling, enabling them to compete with cash offers and buy their next home without contingencies.

---

## What Are Bridge Loans?

**Purpose:** Access home equity for down payment on new purchase before selling current home

**How It Works:**
```
Current Home:
- Value: $700,000
- Mortgage: $300,000
- Equity: $400,000

Bridge Loan:
- Unity lends against $400,000 equity
- Customer uses for down payment on new home
- Current home STAYS ON MARKET
- Qualifies for BOTH mortgages
- Bridge loan is interest-only
```

---

## When Bridge Loans Were Created

**COVID Era Problem:**
- Market so hot homes sold in days
- All-cash offers winning everything
- Traditional buyers couldn't compete
- Needed to sell first = lost deals

**Bridge Loan Solution:**
- Act like cash buyer
- Make non-contingent offer
- Keep current home listed
- Buy time to sell

## When to Suggest Bridge Loans

**Ideal Scenarios:**
1. Substantial equity in current home
2. Can carry two mortgages (even temporarily)
3. Current home priced to sell
4. Found perfect next home (don't want to lose it)
5. Competitive market with multiple offers

**Requirements:**
- Significant equity (enough for down payment)
- Strong income (qualify for both)
- 680+ credit score
- 6-12 months reserves
- Comfortable with temporary risk

## Agent Talking Points

**For Hesitant Customers:**
> "Bridge loans let you compete with cash buyers without the sale contingency that sellers hate. Statistics show homes in our market sell within 60 days. You're only carrying both mortgages temporarily, and the bridge loan is interest-only so payments are manageable. If it's the right house, don't lose it waiting to sell first."

**Cost Analysis:**
```
Scenario: Carry both mortgages 60 days

Current Home Payment: $3,000/month
New Home Payment: $4,000/month
Bridge Loan (Interest-Only): $1,500/month
Total 2 Months: $17,000

Alternative: Lost dream home, settled for less desirable property
Value: Priceless
```

## Key Takeaways

- Bridge loans created during COVID, still valuable today
- Unity allows keeping home on market (rare)
- Interest-only structure keeps costs manageable
- Best for substantial equity + strong income
- Alternative: Home equity loan if not in rush

**Bottom Line:** Bridge loans provide competitive advantage in hot markets. Unity's flexibility (keeping home listed) reduces risk. Work with Pat to structure creatively for customers who need to buy before selling.
