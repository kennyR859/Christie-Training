### Method 1: Direct ChatGPT Search

**The Problem:** ChatGPT knows you if you use it regularly

**The Solution:** Use "Temporary Chat"

**How to Use Temporary Chat:**
1. Open ChatGPT
2. Look for settings/profile icon
3. Select "Temporary Chat" mode
4. This chat won't train on your data
5. AI won't remember your previous conversations

**Test Prompts to Use:**

```
"Who is the best real estate agent in [Your Town]?"

"I'm looking for a real estate agent who specializes in [Your Neighborhood]. Who do you recommend?"

"Who are the top 3 real estate agents in [Your County] for first-time buyers?"

"I need an agent who knows the [School District] area really well. Who should I contact?"

"Recommend a real estate agent for luxury homes in [Your Area]."
```

**What to Look For:**
- Do you appear in results?
- What position are you listed?
- What information does AI share about you?
- What sources does it cite? (Google Business, Zillow, reviews, etc.)
- Are there errors in your information?

---

### Method 2: Have Someone Else Search

**Best Approach:** Ask a friend/family member

**Why This Works:**
- Completely clean search (AI doesn't know you)
- Most realistic to actual client experience
- Can test from different geographic locations

**The Ask:**

Just screenshot what comes up and text it to me. Thanks!"

**Have Them Test Multiple Prompts:**
